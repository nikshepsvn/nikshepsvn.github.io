Welcome Note

February 2025

I've been meaning to start writing more and this is my first step. No concrete plans on what's going to be up here but my guess is it'll be a combination of thoughts & musings on tech, space, spirituality, neuroscience, the human experience, and anything else that grabs my attention.

A little about myself if you don't know me: I'm a 26-year-old builder living in Downtown Toronto. After selling my AI storytelling platform RealmPlay and wrapping up my time as a Senior Software Engineer @ Instacart, I've been heads down building DreamLoom and other exciting projects under Atman Ventures. When I'm not obsessing over new technologies or diving deep into AI research, you'll find me at board game nights or raves with friends.

My journey's been a bit unconventional - from mining ETH at $35 and building viral YouTube channels to engineering data platforms at companies like Coinbase and Instacart. Most recently, I've been fortunate to have two successful exits with RealmPlay and SoulBazaar, both in the AI space. Each venture has been a stepping stone, teaching me invaluable lessons about building products that matter.

Right now, I'm all in on exploring the intersection of AI and creativity. Through DreamLoom and our other ventures, we're building technology that helps people earn through their creativity rather than labor. While I write to share my experiences and realizations, I hope these posts bring you value, spark joy, or offer a new perspective. Welcome to my corner of the internet, and I hope you feel right at home.
